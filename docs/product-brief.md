## **How It Works**

1. **Inputs (connect & configure)**
    - Connect Shopify, GA4, Meta, Google Ads, Klaviyo, etc.
    - Launch survey (brand ESP or hosted link) to recent buyers; capture psychographics, affinities, and product intents.
2. **Persona Intelligence**
    - Cluster customers by value (RFM/LTV/profit) + survey signals to form actionable personas.
    - Map acquisition sources, first-product affinities, and predicted payback per persona.
    - Set a recommended **target mix** (shift spend toward high-value personas).
3. **Creative & Strategy Layer**
    - Generate persona-specific creative briefs: angles, hooks, product pairings, email flows, landing page cues, influencer archetypes.
    - Produce draft ad/email copy and image prompts matched to each persona.
    - Recommend budget reallocation and testing plan (what to scale, what to cut).
4. **Outputs / Deliverables**
    - **Real-time dashboard:** persona share over time, profitability bridge, drift vs target mix, CAC payback by persona.
    - **Creative pack:** briefs + drafts ready for brand review and execution.
    - **Exports:** CSVs of persona labels, recommended segments, and creative artifacts.
5. **Ops & Automation (Agency)**
    - Use **ValueTracker** to baseline current manual hours for persona/reporting/creative prep, then quantify hours saved post-MVP.
